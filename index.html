Jon Sweeney: Aspiring Developer

Background

Greetings! My name is Jonathan Sweeney and I am a student at Epicodus. Computers and I haven’t always gotten along.

I am originally from Nebraska. Thankfully, my parents brought me with them to Portland when I was young. Even though I have travelled across the globe, Portland will always feel like home.

In 2005, I enlisted into the Oregon Army National Guard as an Infantryman. Although I joined the ORNG, I spent most of my time on active duty. Since graduating basic training, I have visited 9 countries, served in Operation Iraqi Freedom, Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation Resolute Support. While serving in Afghanistan, I was put in charge of my unit’s communication equipment. Quickly, I became fascinated how the equipment worked. With some mentorship, I started programming while overseas.

Today

Today, I live by a simple philosophy. Every day, I try to improve myself a little bit more. This can be improving my health, expanding my knowledge, or attempting to improve my character. Most of the time I choose to learn or improve a skill. This philosophy led me to discover Epicodus where I am a current student.
